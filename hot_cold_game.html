HOT/COLD GAME

Let's play a fun treasure hunt! "Hot/Cold" is a game where one person hides an object and gives hints by saying "hot" when you're close and "cold" when you're far. Find the hidden object as quickly as you can using the hints.

What You'll Need

SETUP

Participants: 2

Devices: 1

BLOCKS

1. Planning

This is a game to play with a friend.

Look around wherever you are and pick an object from the place.

But be careful! Don't tell your friend which object you chose. Just think about it.

2. Guessing the Object

Now your friend will place the Capybara in the space and try to guess which object you thought of.

Your friend has to move the Capybara close to the objects. When it gets close to the object you chose, you should say "hot". If it moves away, you should say "cold".

Hint: Your friend can move the Capybara by using and Alternatively, your friend can tap, hold, and grab the Capybara and move it around.

3. Take it Further

Now take turns!

When your friend finds the object, take turns and let your friend choose an object for you to guess.
